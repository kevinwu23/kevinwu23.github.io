I'm a 5th year PhD at Stanford University working with Prof. James Zou. I am fortunate to have been supported by the Stanford Data Science Scholars program.

My research focuses on why AI fails to be effectively deployed in critical settings, especially within in healthcare. My work aims to understand and address these barriers through data-driven methods. Prior to my PhD, I helped start Deep Health, a health AI company. I completed my Master's degree at Harvard University in 2018, and my bachelor's at Duke University in 2015.
